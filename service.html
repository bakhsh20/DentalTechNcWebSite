Ritter Dental products are made in eastern Germany at the Zwönitz production facility. The company also has administrative offices in Baden Württemberg, South Germany. San Antonio, Texas is home to the Ritter Dental USA distribution facility and customer service center. They are dedicated to offering an unparalleled commitment to customer care. They also have trained technicians available to professionally install dental units and equipment throughout the U.S.

For over 39 years, DCI has proudly served our customers around the world. As a family-owned company, they go above and beyond for their customers. That's why they only provide the highest quality dental tools, dental parts, and operatory equipment. From multi-functional parts to innovative new products, they have the wide selection you're looking for at the price to fit your budget.

Supply Clinic was founded in 2014 by Scott, a newly minted periodontist, who found himself struggling to buy supplies at reasonable prices after finishing school. Scott tapped his brother Jacob, a statistician, and the two began putting together an online marketplace for suppliers to compete for dentists' business. Supply Clinic now works with over 130 suppliers to support over 10,000 dental offices across the U.S. In 2020, Supply Clinic expanded its offerings, and now offers a procurement solution for dental offices of all sizes.
